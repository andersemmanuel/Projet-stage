About AIPAS

The Ideas Plus Solidarity Actions Association (AIPAS) was born from the will of committed men and women, united by a deep conviction: every act of solidarity can change a life and help build a fairer world. Since its creation, AIPAS has been committed to supporting vulnerable populations, promoting peace, and strengthening bonds of mutual aid between peoples.

Our main missions focus on access to education, health, clean water, and emergency aid. We intervene where needs are greatest, with children, families in difficulty, and communities affected by poverty or humanitarian crises. Our action aims to offer concrete and lasting solutions, in partnership with local actors and with respect for every culture.

Our values are the foundation of our commitment: solidarity, mutual aid, transparency, and respect for human dignity guide each of our actions. We believe in the strength of the collective, in everyone’s ability to act and inspire change around them.

Who do we help? AIPAS helps children deprived of school, families without access to care or clean water, people displaced or victims of disasters. We stand alongside those who, everywhere in the world, face injustice or exclusion.

How do we act? Thanks to the generosity of our donors and the commitment of our volunteers, we carry out field actions: distribution of school supplies, construction of wells, health prevention campaigns, food and logistical support during crises. Each project is designed to meet real needs and to strengthen the autonomy of beneficiaries.

What makes us unique is our human and transparent approach, our attention to local needs, and our desire to build bridges between cultures. AIPAS is a supportive family, open to all who wish to act for a more fraternal world.
